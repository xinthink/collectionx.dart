Range<T extends num> class

Represents a range of numbers.

Please note that it's not a strict counterpart of the range in mathematics. In fact, to keep things simple, it's just a pair of numbers, marks the beginning and end of the range, respectively. So that it can represent a reverse range easily, e.g., 3 to 1.
